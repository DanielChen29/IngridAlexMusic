About Us

Ingrid and Alex are an acoustic guitar and vocal duo based in Seattle Washington. They enjoy performing in both indoor and outdoor venues in the Puget Sound region. Playing a mix of audience-pleasing covers and original music, their repertoire includes soft rock, alt folk, Americana, and the occasional jazz and blues tunes.
